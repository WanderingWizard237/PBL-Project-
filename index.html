Disaster Management and Resource Allocation System

By Aditya Aggarwal | Reg No: 2427030522

Introduction

A Disaster Management and Resource Allocation System is designed to efficiently handle and coordinate activities before, during, and after a disaster to minimize its impact on people, property, and the environment. It integrates technology, communication, and resource management to ensure timely response and recovery.

Current Systems

Ushahidi

Pros:

  • Rapid crowdsourced reporting (SMS, web, social)
  • Improves situational awareness
  • Easy deployment

Cons:

  • Data quality and verification issues
  • Limited asset-management features

Sahana Eden

Pros:

  • Strong coordination & tracking features
  • Open-source and extensible

Cons:

  • Complex installation
  • Requires technical expertise

GDACS

Pros:

  • Global disaster alerts
  • Satellite & sensor integration

Cons:

  • Coarse global-level data
  • Not a full resource allocation system

Our Solution – DRROS

Disaster Response and Resource Optimization System (DRROS) is designed with AI-based prediction models and optimization algorithms for real-time disaster detection and efficient resource allocation.
